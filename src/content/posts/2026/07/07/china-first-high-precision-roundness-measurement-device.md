# China Builds First High-Precision Roundness Reference Device

China has successfully developed and built its first high-precision roundness reference device, a landmark achievement in precision measurement technology that fills a critical gap in the nation's metrology infrastructure. Developed by the [China Institute of Metrology](https://news.bjd.com.cn/2026/07/06/11848409.shtml), the device reduces roundness measurement uncertainty from 20 nanometers to 6 nanometers, reaching internationally advanced levels and breaking a long-standing foreign technological monopoly.

## Context: The Roundness Measurement Gap

Roundness is one of the core fundamental parameters in the geometric dimensioning and tolerancing (GD&T) system. Its measurement precision directly determines the performance and assembly quality of high-end manufacturing products, including precision spindles, advanced optical components, and semiconductor chips.

Although China had previously established national-level measurement benchmarks for other geometric parameters such as flatness, roundness measurement had long lacked a national-level traceability source. According to multiple Chinese media reports, this gap had become a "bottleneck problem" restricting the independent control of high-end equipment and the high-quality development of strategic industries.

## Technical Breakthrough

The newly completed roundness reference device integrates multiple independently developed innovative technologies, solving international technical bottlenecks in high-accuracy roundness evaluation, spindle error separation, and filter consistency control. As [IT Home](https://www.ithome.com/0/973/328.htm) reported, the device proposes a novel roundness calculation model based on high-accuracy roundness filtering and full-efficiency data utilization, effectively resolving the stability challenge of contour reconstruction after standard hemisphere separation.

A key innovation is the independently developed error separation technology that substantially suppresses spindle rotation error, reducing roundness measurement uncertainty from 20 nanometers to 6 nanometers — a more than threefold improvement. This places China's measurement capability at the internationally advanced level, comparable to leading national metrology institutes worldwide.

## Strategic Importance

The device will serve strategic industries including aerospace, high-end machine tools, advanced optics, and semiconductor manufacturing by providing highly reliable and precise measurement traceability. This achievement aligns with China's broader national strategies of becoming a "Manufacturing Power" and "Quality Power," as well as its push for self-reliance in core technologies.

Previously, Chinese manufacturers had to rely on foreign calibration services or measurement standards for roundness calibration, creating dependence on foreign technology, potential accuracy inconsistencies, higher costs, and vulnerability to export controls. The new device eliminates this dependency, establishing a complete national roundness measurement traceability system for the first time.

## Industry Impact

The implications extend across multiple high-tech sectors. In aerospace, precision roundness measurement is critical for engine components and bearings. In semiconductor manufacturing, it affects wafer processing and chip packaging quality. The optics industry benefits from improved lens manufacturing capabilities, while precision machinery gains more reliable quality control standards.

According to CCTV's "Morning News" program, which featured the achievement on July 7, 2026, the device not only perfects the national geometric measurement system but also empowers the construction of a manufacturing and quality powerhouse with high-precision metrology, injecting strong momentum into the new round of technological development and industrial transformation.

## What's Next

The completion of this device marks a significant milestone, but questions remain about the timeline for deploying this calibration capability to industrial users across China. The achievement positions China to participate more actively in international metrology standards development, potentially influencing global benchmarks for precision measurement. As China continues to push into high-end manufacturing, the metrological foundation established by this breakthrough will be essential for supporting the next generation of precision-engineered products.
