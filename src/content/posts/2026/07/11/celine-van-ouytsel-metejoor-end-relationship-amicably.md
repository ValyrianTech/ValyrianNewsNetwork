# Celine Van Ouytsel and Metejoor End Relationship Amicably

Former Miss Belgium Celine Van Ouytsel (29) and Flemish pop star Metejoor (35) have ended their romantic relationship after approximately ten months together, their management confirmed on Saturday. The couple stated that the decision was mutual and amicable, with no fight or third party involved.

According to a press release issued by their management and reported by [Het Laatste Nieuws](https://www.hln.be/showbizz/celine-van-ouytsel-en-metejoor-zetten-punt-achter-relatie~a76ff4f9/), the split was prompted by diverging future visions. "Their future visions and personal paths unfortunately diverge too much," the statement read. "They look back with great warmth and respect on the beautiful time they shared together."

## A Fairytale Romance

The couple's relationship began in the summer of 2025 when they were frequently spotted together in Knokke. They confirmed their romance in September 2025 on the talk show 'De Tafel van Gert,' where Van Ouytsel described the connection as unexpected, having been single for a long time. The pair quickly became one of Flanders' most high-profile celebrity couples, regularly appearing at major events including Het Gala van de Gouden K's and the MIA awards.

As [Flair](https://www.flair.be/nl/self-love/relatie/celine-van-ouytsel-metejoor-zijn-geen-koppel-meer/) reported, the couple enjoyed a vacation together in Zakynthos, Greece, as recently as June 2026, just weeks before the breakup announcement.

## Signs and Speculation

In the months leading up to the split, observant fans noticed the couple was sharing fewer photos together on social media. Metejoor addressed these concerns in mid-May 2026, telling fans not to worry. "Sometimes you see each other more, sometimes less. Being constantly together isn't healthy either, but people really shouldn't worry," he said.

According to [Redactie24](https://www.redactie24.be/artiesten-vlaanderen/metejoor-en-celine-van-ouytsel-uit-elkaar-192028), there were indications that the couple had differing views on their future together, with Metejoor reportedly wanting to move in together while Van Ouytsel was hesitant.

## Privacy Requested

Both Van Ouytsel and Metejoor have requested privacy during this difficult period. Their management confirmed that no further interviews or public statements about the breakup will be given. "To counter online speculation, but especially to give Celine and Joris the necessary peace and time, we share that it was decided to no longer continue as a couple," the management stated.

## What's Next

Both figures remain active in their respective careers. Metejoor, whose real name is Joris Van Rossem, recently released the single 'Nooduitgang' in May 2026, which reached number one in the Vlaamse Top 30. Van Ouytsel continues her work as a television presenter and radio personality. The story quickly became the most-read and most-shared article on HLN.be, underscoring the significant public interest in the couple's relationship.
