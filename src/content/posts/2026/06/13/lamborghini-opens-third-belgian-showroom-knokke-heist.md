# Lamborghini Opens Third Belgian Showroom in Knokke-Heist

Lamborghini has opened its third showroom in Belgium, choosing the upscale coastal town of Knokke-Heist for its latest expansion. CEO Stephan Winkelmann personally attended the official opening ceremony on the Natiënlaan, the main access road into the seaside resort, as reported by [Het Laatste Nieuws](https://www.hln.be/knokke-heist/waar-anders-dan-in-knokke-heist-lamborghini-opent-derde-showroom-in-ons-land-het-is-een-strategische-locatie-voor-ons-merk~acde9156/).

## A Strategic Location for the Luxury Brand

The new showroom joins existing Lamborghini dealerships in Antwerp and Brussels, bringing the Italian automaker's Belgian retail network to three locations. The choice of Knokke-Heist is no coincidence — the coastal municipality has long been synonymous with wealth, exclusivity, and an international clientele drawn to its luxury shopping, fine dining, and high-end real estate.

"Belgium remains an important market for Lamborghini in Europe and Knokke is a strategic location for our brand," Winkelmann said during the opening. "Known for its exclusive lifestyle and passion for luxury and performance cars, Knokke offers the ideal environment to strengthen Lamborghini's presence in Belgium. With this new dealer, we want to be even closer to our customers while supporting our ongoing expansion in Western Europe."

The Natiënlaan has developed into a premier automotive destination in Belgium, already hosting showrooms for Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, and BMW. Lamborghini's arrival further cements this corridor as the country's go-to hub for luxury car buyers.

## Operated by D'Ieteren Group

The new Lamborghini Knokke dealership is operated by D'Ieteren Group, a major Belgian automotive and mobility company that serves as the official importer for several automotive brands in the country. Michiam Van den Langenbergh serves as General Director of the new location.

"The opening of Lamborghini Knokke represents an important milestone in our development in Belgium," Van den Langenbergh said. "Knokke is a unique destination with an international clientele and a strong affinity with luxury and performance brands. This new location allows us to offer our customers an even closer and more exclusive Lamborghini experience."

## A Glimpse at the Latest Models

During the official opening, guests were treated to a display of Lamborghini's latest lineup, including a deep red Revuelto — the flagship V12 hybrid plug-in supercar that succeeds the Aventador — the new Urus SE plug-in hybrid SUV, and the recently unveiled Temerario, a hybrid V8 successor to the Huracán.

## Personalization at the Core

The showroom features an 'Ad Personam' personalization studio, where clients can customize virtually every detail of their vehicle, from stitching and leather finishes to bespoke wheels and exclusive paint colors. This high-value service offering reflects a broader industry trend toward personalized luxury experiences and generates additional revenue beyond vehicle sales.

## Broader Market Implications

Lamborghini's expansion into Knokke-Heist signals strong confidence in the Belgian luxury automotive market. The move comes as the luxury automotive sector continues to grow, with increasing demand for high-end SUVs like the Urus and hybrid-electrified performance vehicles. Lamborghini has been aggressively expanding its retail footprint in Western Europe, and the Knokke location positions the brand to serve both local Belgian customers and the international affluent visitors who flock to the coastal town.

## What to Watch For

The new showroom is expected to attract additional affluent visitors to Knokke-Heist, potentially benefiting local hospitality and retail businesses. It also raises questions about further expansion: with three showrooms now in Belgium, industry observers will be watching whether Lamborghini plans additional locations in neighboring markets or further deepens its presence in the Benelux region.

For now, the message from Lamborghini is clear: Belgium remains a cornerstone of its European strategy, and Knokke-Heist — with its unique blend of coastal glamour and international cachet — is the perfect place to make that statement.
