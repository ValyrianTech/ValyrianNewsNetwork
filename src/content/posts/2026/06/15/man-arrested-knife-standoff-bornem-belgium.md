# Man Arrested After Knife Standoff in Bornem, Belgium

Special units of the Belgian Federal Police arrested a 23-year-old man on Sunday evening in Bornem, Antwerp province, after he barricaded himself inside a residence armed with a knife. The standoff, which lasted several hours, was resolved without injuries, according to the Antwerp Public Prosecutor's Office.

The incident began when local police officers arrived at a residence on Houtemolenstraat to arrest the man, who was wanted to serve a prison sentence for drug-related offenses. When officers attempted to take him into custody, he refused to comply, retreated into the home, and displayed a knife.

## Police Response and Standoff

Facing an armed and barricaded suspect, local officers from Police Zone Rivierenland made the tactical decision to withdraw for safety reasons. The Special Units of the Federal Police ("Speciale Eenheden van de Federale Politie") were called in to handle the escalating situation.

According to [VRT NWS](https://www.vrt.be/vrtnws/nl/2026/06/15/bornem-man-met-mes-opgepakt-verschanst/), a safety perimeter was established around the residence in the residential neighborhood as the special units took over the intervention. The operation lasted several hours as authorities worked to resolve the situation peacefully.

Kristof Aerts, spokesperson for the Antwerp Public Prosecutor's Office, confirmed the sequence of events. "The man did not want to voluntarily go with the police and retreated into his home," Aerts told [HLN](https://www.hln.be/bornem/man-23-verschanst-zich-urenlang-met-mes-in-woning-in-bornem-nadat-politie-hem-wil-arresteren~a96af263/). "During the intervention, he showed a knife, after which the police officers present withdrew for safety reasons."

## Resolution

The standoff came to an end in the evening when the special units successfully arrested the man without further incident. "After an intervention by the special units, the man could be arrested," Aerts said. "The operation proceeded without further incidents and there were no injuries."

Following his arrest, the man was transported to prison to serve his sentence for drug-related offenses. His identity has not been publicly released by authorities.

## Context and Background

Bornem is a municipality of approximately 21,000 residents located along the Scheldt River in the province of Antwerp, in Belgium's Flemish Region. The Houtemolenstraat, where the incident occurred, is a residential street in the town.

The Special Units of the Belgian Federal Police are elite tactical units comparable to SWAT teams in other countries. They are deployed for high-risk interventions including barricaded suspects, hostage situations, and armed confrontations. Their involvement in this case indicates that local police assessed the situation as too dangerous for a conventional response.

This incident is part of a pattern of barricade situations in Belgium requiring specialized police intervention. In May 2026, special units in Holsbeek (Flemish Brabant) overpowered a man who had barricaded himself with a hammer after a four-hour standoff. Similar incidents have occurred in Brugge (2017), Burcht (2018), Kortrijk (2020), and Gent (2020), where armed individuals barricaded themselves and required tactical response.

## Analysis

The successful resolution of the Bornem standoff highlights the effectiveness of Belgium's tiered police response system. Local officers appropriately withdrew upon encountering an armed suspect, allowing specialized units with advanced training and equipment to handle the high-risk situation. The establishment of a safety perimeter demonstrated concern for both officer and public safety in the residential area.

The suspect's decision to display a knife when faced with arrest escalated what would have been a routine apprehension into a high-risk tactical situation. His subsequent arrest without injuries reflects the professionalism of the special units in managing such volatile encounters.

## What's Next

The suspect is now serving his prison sentence for drug offenses. Authorities have not disclosed the specific length of his sentence or whether additional charges will be filed related to the standoff. The incident serves as a reminder of the unpredictable nature of police work and the critical role specialized tactical units play in ensuring public safety.
